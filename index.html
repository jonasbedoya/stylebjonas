About me

15 years ago I started my journey in the hair and beauty industry in the beautiful Barcelona and, for the last 3 years I have been working in another gorgeous city of Amsterdam... Hairdressing is not just about cutting and coloring your hair. I have a passion for hairstyling and the artistry that comes with it. What makes my clients comfortable is my passion, dedication, hard work and ability to customize your salon experience to fit your needs. An essential part of my job is the consultation where I establish what my clients want. During my treatment I use the best Davines products that will make your hair stronger, shiny, adding volume and sleekness. I use a wide pallet of styling and coloring techniques to make your hair look rich and stylish.
